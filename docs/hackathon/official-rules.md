### Judging and Post-Event Video Submission

Hackathon Competitor Track Judging: Workday will review all entries for compliance with these Official Rules. All eligible submissions will be judged by a panel of judges composed of Workday selected judges based on the following judging criteria (“Judging Criteria”):

* Use of Platform
* Innovation
* Business Value
* Wow Factor

Ten (10) teams will be finalists. Of the finalists, one (1) finalist will be the overall winner and two (2) finalists will be the runners up. The judging and final determination of the finalists will be in the sole discretion of the judges. In order to be judged and in order to win a prize, a team must have at least two in-person participants.

Each entry must be original, of the participant’s or participant’s team’s own creation and newly written during the Hackathon and must not have been entered in any other competition or program similar to the Hackathon. “Newly written” means that participants cannot copy or modify an existing program, whether written by them or by a third party, and submit it as part of an entry.

Post-Event Video Submission: Following the live event, Competitor Track teams will be invited to submit a video presentation of their project for a public "People’s Choice" vote. Voting will be open to the public on a Workday-designated platform. Workday reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to disqualify any votes that appear to be generated by scripts, bots, or any fraudulent means.

### Prizes (Competitor Track Only)

* First Prize: $500 gift for each team member, up to five registered participants.
* Second Prize: $300 gift for each team member, up to five registered participants.
* Third Prize: $200 gift for each team member, up to five registered participants.
* Crowd Vote Award: $50 Workday Merchandise Store Credit, up to five registered participants.
* Post-Event Video Prize: $100 prize for each team member of the team receiving the most public votes, up to five registered participants.

Note: Learner Track participants are not eligible for the prizes listed above.
