For DevCon 2026 Hackathon, Workday has partnered with Snowflake and Salesforce who have provided access to their tenants.

To use Data Cloud during the Hackathon, registered hackers can use Workday, Snowflake, and Salesforce tenants.

## Workday Tenant Access

Access information for Workday tenants during the Hackathon are provided by the Developer Relations team.  

## Snowflake Tenant Access

To access a Snowflake tenant during the Hackathon:

1. In your Hackathon team, assign 1 email account as the Snowflake account.
2. Click [this Snowflake registration link](https://go.dataops.live/workday-devcon-conference-new/register) to sign up for your team.
3. Enter your team’s email account.

You’ll receive a link to your Snowflake tenant and a temporary password. You can change the password in Snowflake when you sign in.

> Note: During the hackathon, we encourage you to record videos of your project as soon as possible. Snowflake tenants will be available for hackathon development until Jun 20, 2026.

## Salesforce Tenant Access

To access a Salesforce tenant during the Hackathon:

1. Obtain the username and password assigned to your team by the Hackathon facilitator.
2. Click [this Salesforce login page](https://login.salesforce.com/).
3. Enter the username and password assigned to your team.
4. After a successful login, access Data Cloud:

  1. Locate the App Selector in the upper right hand corner.
  2. Enter 'Data Cloud'.

> Note: During the hackathon, we encourage you to record videos of your project as soon as possible. Salesforce tenants will be available for hackathon development until Jun 5, 2026.
