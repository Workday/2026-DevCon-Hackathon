Configurable ConnectR is a multi-step experience that guides users through complex Workday business processes, such as Change Job, Hire, and Performance Review. Historically, Workday delivered these flows as fixed, unchangeable processes. Configurable ConnectR enables administrators to insert their own custom steps, powered by Extend tasks, directly inside a Workday-delivered flow. From the end user's perspective, the Extend step appears as a standard Workday step, sharing the same navigation, the Review & Submit step, and other interface elements.

This capability supports various use cases, including:

* Inserting a "Right to Work" Extend app into the Hire flow only for employees hired in the UK.
* Adding a custom data capture step in Change Job before the compensation section.
* Embedding an instructions step into Performance Review.
