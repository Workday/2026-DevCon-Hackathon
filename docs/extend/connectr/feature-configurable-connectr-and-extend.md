## Overview
Configurable ConnectR guides users through complex Workday business processes, such as Change Job, Hire, and Performance Review, using a multi-step experience. Historically, Workday delivered these flows as fixed, unchangeable processes. Configurable ConnectR enables administrators to insert their own custom steps, which Extend tasks power, directly inside a Workday-delivered flow. The Extend step appears as a standard Workday step to the end user, sharing the same navigation, the Review & Submit step, and other interface elements.
