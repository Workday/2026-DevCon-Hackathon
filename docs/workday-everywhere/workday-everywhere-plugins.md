Workday Everywhere plugins allow developers to surface Workday Extend marketplace apps directly within Workday Everywhere surfaces, such as Microsoft Teams, Slack, and Gemini. This feature enables developer-built functionality to exist as plugins to the traditional Workday app, delivering additional value by meeting users in their natural flow of work.
